Regular Expressions

We want to match strings. This is what we call RegEx usually.

There are 3 operations that we can do:

  • Concatenation: or , matches the concatenation of two characters, in two consecutive spots.
  • Union: , matches both characters at a single spot.
  • Star: or (for any character), matches a sequence of any length of a character.
Note
  • means no character, just like pressing enter without any character.
  • stands for the alphabet, so it's a placeholder for any character in the alphabet.
Hint

In the definitions above i just mention characters, but beware, we can input a sequence of characters in place of a single character for any of those operations.

The string above matches any string that has even length.
