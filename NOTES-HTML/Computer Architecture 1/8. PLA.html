8. PLA

We refer to the traditional circuits as multi-level logic.
But we also have something called two-level logic (SOP & POS forms), or PLA (Programmable Logic Array), which is a circuit in which we have a level of all ANDs and a level of all ORs.

Hint

It's basically a SOP made with a circuit.
It's called two-level logic because we have two layers with all the same gates.
