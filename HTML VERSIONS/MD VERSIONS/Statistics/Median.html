The median is the middle value of the observations when they are ordered from the smallest to the largest.

Steps to find median:

  1. Order the observations;
  2. Check the even/odd number thing;
  3. Get the median.

Info

The median is more robust to extreme values than the mean, because the median doesn't take into considerations all the values.

Skewed Distributions

  • Median == Mean: Symmetrical
  • Median < Mean: Skewed to the right
  • Median > Mean: Skewed to the left
