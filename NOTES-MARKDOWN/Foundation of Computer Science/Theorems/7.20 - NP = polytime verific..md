> [!hint] Hint by the professor
> We have two definitions of NP problems.
> On one side, we have the whole tree of non-deterministic guesses.
> On the other side, we have the verifier, and we take into consideration only one branch.
> We show that the solutions given by those two models are the same.

## $\rightarrow$

We need to construct the verifier from the existing Non-Deterministic TM that accepts it.


## $\leftarrow$

We need to construct the Non-Deterministic TM from an existing verifier.
