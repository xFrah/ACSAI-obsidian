Energy (Joules) transferred between two systems due to a [[0. Internal energy - Temperature|temperature]] difference.
It is energy that flows from the region of higher temperature to the region of lower temperature and **not a property a system possesses on its own**.

Heat contributes to changes in a system's [[0. Internal energy - Temperature|internal energy]].

In a mechanical sense, it's the transfer of kinetic energy from one set of particles to another.

> [!hint] Difference between Heat and Temperature
> - **Temperature**: the _average_ kinetic energy per particle. It tells you how fast the "typical" particle is moving.
> - **Heat**: the _total_ amount of kinetic energy that actually moves from the hot object to the cold one.
