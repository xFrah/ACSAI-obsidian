Work is a scalar quantity and it's defined as an integral of the force times the distance:

$$\large W = \int \vec{F}\cdot \vec{dr} = \int |\bar{F}||\vec{d}r|\cos\theta=|\bar{F}|\cos\theta \Delta r$$

It's measured in Joules.
